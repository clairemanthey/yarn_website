Who are we?

Located in the College Square Shopping Center, Yarns Untold is the western suburbs friendliest place to find your knitting and crochet needs. We look to ignite your ideas to help bring your creative stories to life. Whether your passion is socks, sweaters, heirloom items or a must have fun project, Yarns Untold has something for you.

Hand-dyed, kettle dyed, custom created... amid a rainbow of colors and fibers, you are bound to fall in love with something.

Don't see what you are looking for, whether a pattern or a yarn, please let us know. We are here to help.
